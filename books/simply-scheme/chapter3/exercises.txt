- 3.9:
 The result of the expression (+ 8 2) is 10.
 - As an atom: 10
 - Another compound expression made up of three atoms: (+ 1 9)
 - A compound expression made up of four atoms: (+ 2 2 6)
 - A compound expression made up of an atom and two compound subexpressions:
   (+ (* 2 3) (* 2 2))
 - Any other kind of expression: (sqrt 100)
